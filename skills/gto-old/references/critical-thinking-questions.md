# Critical Thinking Questions

This file contains questions for critical thinking analysis.

## Questions for /reflect Integration

1. **What assumptions am I making?**
   - Are the detected errors real or false positives?
   - Is the context sufficient for accurate analysis?

2. **What could go wrong?**
   - What edge cases are not covered?
   - What failure modes exist in the current approach?

3. **What is the quality of the evidence?**
   - Are the findings grounded in actual transcript data?
   - Are there any hallucinated or speculative claims?

4. **What am I missing?**
   - What patterns might be overlooked?
   - What additional context would improve analysis?

5. **How could this be better?**
   - What improvements would increase accuracy?
   - What additional analysis would be valuable?
